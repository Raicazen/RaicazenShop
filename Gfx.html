REMEMBER: This samples are simply pre-samples, the images provided are unfinished products/commisions. WHAT IS A GFX? GFX is an abbreviation of graphics, which refers to visual images or designs on a surface. GFX can also mean graphic art or graphic design, which are creative processes of producing visual communication and expression. GFX is a common term used on social media platforms, such as Snapchat, Instagram, and TikTok, as well as online games, such as Roblox.
